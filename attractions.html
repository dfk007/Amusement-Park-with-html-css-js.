ATTRACTIONS

THE FERRIS WHEEL

What is so funny about a ferris wheel? well, nothing! except when you make sure it cracks, fall down and goes rolling down the street, jumps in the air and falls to safety, on a moody lake full with hungry crocs! You will never see ferris wheels as a boring thing again! $29.95 a ride. Bring your gator gun.

THE NIGHT TOUR

Oh, no, nononono...the witch of the old cabin in the woods... no... you won't survive this. Please, no! Well, we can't properly describe this attraction because no one has came back alive to tell us how it is... Are you brave enough to endure? See you tonight at the old cemetery, 12:00 sharp. Bring $29.99 with you.

THE ROLLER COASTER OF HELL

Oh, dear visitor, Have you ever listened to "Higway to Hell", actually going to hell? This is the roller coaster that gives the name to the park, the oldest attraction, therefore, the one with the more deaths at its account. only the bravest of the bravest have survived. We promise, in case of death, we honor you with our own version of the darwin award... you came knowing the dangers, right? $15 a ride+$10 life insurance.

THE MAGIC CASTLE OF HORRORS

Did we say death? yeah, death! the dead enjoy a deal with the devil so they can live in the castle with other creeps and creatures of the darkness. Don't come alone, you will need somebody to recognize your remains when the police come again for the regular dead inspection. This is not treasure hunt, but let me tell you, they say that on the cellar there is a golden treasure hidden. Unfortunately the only thing treasure hunters find, is a horrible death. $39.99, after signing our life insurance policy.

THE CIRCUS

"Come.." says the blood thirsty, pointy theethed, sick looking clown. "come... I just want to tear your legs apart and hit you in the head with it.." ...don't you worry, authorities didn't allow us this kind of show, but yo can see our beloved demonic clown perform the action with cookie men and ketchup as blood.. yeah.. ketchup! $10.99, shows every 1/2 hour

THE SLOWEST CARROUSEL

This is actually slow. Regular marry-go-round music mounted on a colorful little horse. No one can hurt you here... except Mike. Yes, that bully from high school, remember? Yes, we have him... we have them all! We have Mike, and Charlie, and that ugly giant kid, Ben, too! When you thought there was nothing horribly scary about carrousels, we have the slowest one. enjoy turning around at the slowest speed while your school-time bullies throw banana peels and rotten apples and rotten eggs to you. enjoy while they remember you the worst season of your life. But there is some poetic justice on it... what a job they got, right? $10.99 for 2 rounds (two hours)
